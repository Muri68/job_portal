Our Story

How we started our journey to transform the job search process

TECH RECRUITMENT UK was founded in 2015 with a simple mission: to make job searching and hiring more efficient, transparent, and human. What started as a small platform has grown into a comprehensive job marketplace connecting thousands of employers with top talent.

Our team of dedicated professionals works tirelessly to improve our platform, adding new features and functionality to serve both job seekers and employers better. We believe that everyone deserves to find work they're passionate about and that companies should have access to the best talent.

Today, TECH RECRUITMENT UK serves over 1 million users monthly and has helped facilitate more than 100,000 successful job placements across various industries and career levels.
